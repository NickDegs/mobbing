## 📧 OUTREACH NOTE — ENGLISH (human-written)

**Subject:** I got mobbed at work, so I made a game about it

Hi,

My name is Baris, I'm a solo developer from Turkey. English is not my first language so sorry for any mistakes.

Short version of my story: two weeks into a new job I caught a bad cold. I went to a state hospital and got an official sick report, approved by the Ministry of Health. They rejected it. A government medical report - rejected. So I kept working while sick.

It gets better: because I was sick I had to use the bathroom all the time, so I had to start my break early. The result? A penalty - extra hours after my shift. I literally got punished for being sick. Then I got yelled at in front of everyone. So I quit.

Instead of staying quiet I made a game about it. It's called MOBBING - a swipe card game about workplace psychological harassment. You play the manager, the bosses want you to crush people, and if you don't, they crush you. I wanted everyone to feel that machine for five minutes.

159 decision cards based on real mobbing research (the Leymann typology). I translated it into 16 languages and built everything alone. It's live on the App Store: [LINK]

No ads, fully offline, I didn't make it for money. There is also an info corner inside for people who face mobbing in real life - because when it happened to me, nobody told me my rights.

If you think this is worth writing about, I'm happy to answer any questions - I still have all the documents, including the medical report. Press images attached.

Thanks for reading,
Baris
daclen10@icloud.com
