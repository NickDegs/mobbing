APP REVIEW NOTES — MOBBING v1.1.0

Dear Review Team,

This is a content/gameplay update to an already-approved app (v1.0.0). No new
data is collected, the app remains fully offline, has no ads, and no account
or login is required.

WHAT CHANGED IN THIS UPDATE
• A new "Health" stat and a burnout ending were added.
• An "evidence & lawsuit" system: the player can gather in-game proof against
  the fictional company and, if strong enough, pursue a fictional court case.
  Collecting evidence carries an in-game risk of being "caught."
• Difficulty now scales with in-game days; the fictional company grows harder.
• End-of-game summaries reflect the player's choices and their meaning under
  general labor-law concepts (educational/awareness framing, no legal advice).
• At the end of every game a short, supportive message is shown for anyone who
  may be experiencing workplace harassment in real life, encouraging them to
  document incidents, confide in someone they trust, and learn their rights.

ABOUT THE THEME
MOBBING is a satirical card game about workplace psychological harassment,
based on the solo developer's own real experience. All companies, characters,
and events in the game are fictional. The tone is critical/satirical of abusive
workplace practices and is intended to build empathy and awareness. It does not
target, name, or defame any real person or organization.

IN-APP PURCHASE
The existing consumable IAP ("Pay a Bribe" — continue after an ending) is
unchanged from the approved version. Nothing about pricing or purchase flow
was modified.

LOCALIZATION & DEVICES
The update is fully localized in 16 languages. All new UI (health bar, evidence
badge, lawsuit card, end-of-game screens) uses adaptive layouts and has been
verified to fit every supported iPhone screen size, including the latest models;
end-of-game screens are scrollable to avoid any clipping on smaller devices.

Thank you for reviewing.
— Barış (solo developer)
